Quick Information

About HPTU

Established in 2010, HPTU is the premier technical university of Himachal Pradesh, affiliating engineering, pharmacy and management colleges across the state.

Programs Offered

B.Tech, M.Tech, BBA, MBA, BCA, MCA, B.Pharmacy, M.Pharmacy, Diploma and various other technical programs.

Examinations

University conducts end-semester exams for all affiliated colleges. Results and date sheets available on the official website.

Admissions

Admissions through HPCET, JEE Main, GATE, MAT/CMAT. Lateral entry available for diploma holders into B.Tech programs.
